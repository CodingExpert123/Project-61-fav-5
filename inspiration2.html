Carlsen participated in the sixth edition of Shamkir Chess, held from 31 March to 9 April. In late May, Carlsen won his sixth tournament in a row, winning the Lindores Abbey Chess Stars Tournament. Carlsen competed in the 8th Norway Chess from 5 to 16 October, which was the first elite over-the-board tournament held since social restrictions related to the COVID-19 pandemic were enforced
